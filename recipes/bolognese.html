My Bolognese Recipe

This is my favourite Bolognese recipe, it is one that I use every week as it is one of my favourite meals.

This is a simple to make recipe that can be done using supermarket ingrediants.


Ingredients

  • Pasta/Spaghetti
  • Onions
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomatos

Steps

  1. Prep the Ingredients.
  2. Put the ingredients into the slow cooker.
  3. Leave to cook for 4-6 hours.
  4. Ensure it is piping hot in the middle before serving.
