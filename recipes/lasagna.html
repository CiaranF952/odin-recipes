My Lasagna Recipe

This is my favourite lasagna recipe, it is one that I use every week as it is one of my favourite meals.

This is a simple to make recipe that can be done using supermarket ingrediants.


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Lasagna Sauce
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomatos

Steps

  1. Prep the Ingredients.
  2. Form the layers in an oven proof dish.
  3. Place in the oven for 1 hour.
  4. Ensure it is piping hot in the middle before serving.
