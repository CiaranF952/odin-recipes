My Carbonara Recipe

This is my favourite Carbonara recipe, it is one that I use every week as it is one of my favourite meals.

This is a simple to make recipe that can be done using supermarket ingrediants.


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ham Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the Ingredients.
  2. Place into a well oiled pan.
  3. Stir until sauce thickens.
  4. Ensure it is piping hot in the middle before serving.
